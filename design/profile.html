Early Political activities Started attending Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) training sessions at the age of 8.

Became a full time campaigner for RSS in 1971

Arresting for Involving in a non-violent protest in New Delhi, against the Indian government.

Appointed as General Secretary of the “Gujarat Lok Sangharsh Samiti”, an RSS committee which was coordinating the oppositions in Gujrat against the emergency decarled by the then Prime Minister, Indira Gandhi.

Became an RSS regional organiser in 1978

Joined Bhartiya Janta Party (BJP) in 1985

In1987, helped organise the BJP’s campaign in the Ahmedabad municipal election, resulting BJP won easily.

In 1987, elected as the General Secretary of the BJP’s Gujarat unit later in 1987.

Seleted as a member of the BJP’s National Election Committee in 1990, to help L. K. Advani to organise Ram Rath Yatra and Murli Manohar Joshi’s 1991–92 Ekta Yatra.

In 1995, he was appointed as the National Secretary of the party and given the charge of five major states in India

Became Chief Minister of Gujrat on October, 3, 2001 by repalcing Keshubhai patel.

Due to the allegations of Gujrat riots 2002, submitted resignation on April 2002 at BJP national executive meeting in Goa, which was not accepted by the party.

Second term of Modi Govt. started on December, 22, 2002.

Won Gujrat Assembly elections and elected again as the Chief Minister of Gujrat in 2007.

Retained as the Chief Minister by winning Gujrat elections in 2012.
